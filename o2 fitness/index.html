About Us

Ongole's
Oxi-Gen

O2 Fitness is Ongole's brand-new, state-of-the-art premium gym located on the 3rd floor at Pandaripuram, Mangamuru Road — featuring Ongole's first dedicated open terrace workout space.

Built for serious fitness enthusiasts and beginners alike, our unisex facility combines advanced equipment, professional trainers, and a spacious, AC-cooled environment — all designed to fuel your transformation from the very first rep.

Rated 4.9★ on Google with 107+ reviews and 4.9/5 on Justdial, O2 Fitness is already Ongole's most trusted fitness destination — and we're just getting started.
